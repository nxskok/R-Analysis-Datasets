Package: mblm
Type: Package
Title: Median-Based Linear Models
Version: 0.11
Date: 2007-07-23
Author: Lukasz Komsta <luke@novum.am.lublin.pl>
Maintainer: Lukasz Komsta <luke@novum.am.lublin.pl>
Description: This package provides linear models based 
 on Theil-Sen single median and Siegel repeated 
 medians. They are very robust (29 or 50 percent breakdown
 point, respectively), and if no outliers are present,
 the estimators are very similar to OLS.
